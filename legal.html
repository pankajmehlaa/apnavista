Apna Vista - Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Apna Vista! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our society management app. By using our platform, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and society details during registration. Payment information when you make transactions through the app.
  • Non-Personal Information: Device information, app usage statistics, and IP addresses for performance improvement.
  • Sensitive Data: Visitor management data, such as guest names and entry details, securely stored and used only for society management purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is utilized to:

  • Facilitate society management, including visitor entries, complaint resolution, and payment processing.
  • Send important notifications and updates related to your society.
  • Improve app features and provide a personalized experience.
  • Ensure security and compliance with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data in the following cases:

  • With Your Society: Admins and guards may access relevant data for operational purposes.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting with payment processing, app analytics, and other services.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our users and platform.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the protection of your data. Measures include:

  • Encryption for sensitive information.
  • Regular audits and updates to maintain app security.
  • Limited access to data by authorized personnel only.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login information.
  • Track app performance and improve usability.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect your app experience.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request the removal of your personal data, subject to legal requirements.
  • Consent Withdrawal: Opt-out of specific data uses, such as marketing communications.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via the app or email. Your continued use of Apna Vista signifies your acceptance of the updated terms.
