Apna Vista - Privacy Policy

  • Privacy Policy

    Apna Vista is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data while providing our society management services. By using our platform, you agree to the terms of this policy.

    • Types of Data We Collect

      We collect data to provide and enhance our services. This includes: - Personal information like your name, contact details, and society name during registration. - Transactional data such as payment details for society fees or amenity bookings. - App usage data like visitor entry logs and complaint records to ensure seamless functionality.

    • How We Use Your Data

      - To manage visitor entries, society payments, complaints, and other functionalities. - To enhance security by sharing visitor information with guards. - To send important notifications related to your society. - For analytics and improvements of our app and services.

    • Sharing Your Data

      We may share your data with: - Society administrators to facilitate management. - Third-party service providers who assist in app functionalities (e.g., payment processors). - Authorities when required by law for compliance or security purposes.

    • Cookies and Tracking

      We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies help us understand user preferences, optimize performance, and provide personalized content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

    • Your Rights

      You have the right to: - Access, update, or delete your personal data. - Withdraw consent for data usage where applicable. - File a complaint if you believe your data has been misused. For any concerns or requests, please contact us at info@apnavista.com.
